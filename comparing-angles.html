Superimposition & Circular Paper Method
📖 From Ganit Prakash Ch 2: We can compare two angles WITHOUT measuring degrees. Two methods — Superimposition and the Circular Paper Method.

🔁 Method 1 — Superimposition

1
Draw two angles on paper.
2
Match the vertices — place both corners on top of each other.
3
Match one arm — align one ray of each angle.
4
The angle whose second arm is further out is bigger!
5
Both arms overlap → angles are equal.

⭕ Method 2 — Circular Paper

1
Take a circular piece of transparent paper.
2
Place its centre on the vertex of the first angle.
3
Mark where the arms cross the circle.
4
Place the same circle on the second angle.
5
The angle with the longer arc is bigger!

📏 Key Rules

✅ Arm length does NOT matter! Only the opening/spread between arms counts.

✅ Position does NOT matter! A tilted angle has the same size as one standing upright.

✅ Both methods give the same answer — superimposition needs both angles together; circular method works on each separately.
