🔺 Why "Triangular Numbers"?

When you arrange the dots, they form a triangle shape! The sequence 1, 3, 6, 10, 15 … Each new number adds one more row at the bottom. The n-th triangular number = 1+2+3+…+n.

🟦 Why "Square Numbers"?

The dots form a perfect square! 1×1=1, 2×2=4, 3×3=9, 4×4=16 … The n-th square number = n×n = n². That's why we say "4 squared" = 16!

✨ The Odd Number Secret!

Square numbers are always the sum of consecutive odd numbers! This is one of the most beautiful patterns in all of mathematics!

🟣 Why "Cube Numbers"?

Cube numbers come from building a 3D cube! 1×1×1=1, 2×2×2=8, 3×3×3=27 … The n-th cube = n³. That's why we say "3 cubed" = 27!

🌟 The 36 Discovery — A Number with Two Identities!

36 = T₈ (8th triangular number) because 1+2+3+4+5+6+7+8 = 36
36 = 6² (square number) because 6×6 = 36
